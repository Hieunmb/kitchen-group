Angara is an online store that sells kitchenware.

We supply a comprehensive range of home appliances to enhance any kitchen. We specialize in kitchen hoods, hobs, ovens, microwaves, slow cookers, dishwashers, and food waste disposers. We are dedicated to providing better kitchen appliances for consumers in a wide range of product categories. Through continuous innovation, our kitchen appliance division always offers optimal cooking solutions.

Through a robust operational and payment support system, the Angana commerce platform is designed to give users a smooth, safe, and quick experience while purchasing online.

Our objective

We are motivated to connect the community of buyers and sellers by offering an e-commerce platform because we believe in the transformative power of technology and want to help make the world a better place.

Our Direction

Angana provides customers in the area with an integrated online shopping experience that includes a large selection of items, a vibrant user community, and a smooth service chain.
